--- SYSTEM & SESSION ---
[mod]+Shift+c    : Reload Sway Config
[mod]+Shift+e    : Exit Sway (Logout)
[mod]+F11        : Blackout Screen Toggle
[Print]          : Take Screenshot

--- LAUNCHERS ---
[mod]+Enter      : Open Terminal (foot)
[mod]+d          : Open App Launcher (dmenu)
[mod]+?          : Show this Hotkey Menu

--- NAVIGATION & WORKSPACES ---
[mod]+[h/j/k/l]  : Focus Window (Vim-style)
[alt]+j          : Switch to Previous Workspace
[alt]+k          : Switch to Next Workspace
[mod]+[1-4]      : Switch to Workspace 1-4

--- WINDOW MANAGEMENT ---
[mod]+Shift+q    : Kill Focused Window
[mod]+Shift+hjkl : Move Window Position
[mod]+v          : Split Vertically (Next app goes BELOW)
[mod]+b          : Split Horizontally (Next app goes SIDEWAYS)

--- AUDIO & BRIGHTNESS ---
F10/F11/F12      : Volume Mute / Down / Up
MicMute Key      : Toggle Microphone
Brightness Keys  : Adjust Screen Light
